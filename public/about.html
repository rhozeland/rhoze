Our history

What began as a vision became a studio, and now stands as a launchpad for the boldest creators

2016

The origin story

Rhozeland was born from a group of friends with a shared passion for creative arts and culture, starting as a small community built on collaboration and self-expression.

2021

Building the creative hub

We grew into a digital platform offering resources, connections, and services that helped creators bring their ideas to life, building a loyal network through our first Rhozeland hub.

2025

Powering the next era

Now, with a fully equipped studio, a credit-based subscription model, and an expanded suite of services, we're a creative engine designed to fuel every stage of a creator's journey.

Rhozeland is now a tokenized public company.

Now, anyone can be a part of Rhozeland through ownership of our official token. The solution is giving it all back — a chance to finally own something meaningful.
