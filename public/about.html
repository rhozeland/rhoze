2016

The origin story

Rhozeland was born from a group of friends with a shared passion for creative arts and culture, starting as a small community built on collaboration and self-expression.

2021

Building the creative hub

We grew into a digital platform offering resources, connections, and services that helped creators bring their ideas to life, building a loyal network through our first Rhozeland hub.

2023

Studio launch

We opened our first physical studio space — a creative hub for recording, production, and collaboration under one roof.

2025

Powering the next era

Now, with a fully equipped studio, a credit-based subscription model, and an expanded suite of services, we're a creative engine designed to fuel every stage of a creator's journey.

The Flywheel

How the ecosystem works

A self-reinforcing creative economy where every action — creating, proving, spending — powers the next.

01

Create & Contribute

Post to Flow, complete milestones, review peers, or join Drop Rooms. Every meaningful action earns $RHOZE credits automatically.

02

Build Reputation

Each reward generates a Contribution Proof — a tamper-proof record of your work. Anchor it on Solana for a portable, verifiable creative identity.

03

Spend & Unlock

Use $RHOZE to book studios, hire talent, and purchase marketplace offerings. Higher token tiers unlock premium perks and visibility.

04

Revenue Sharing

When your work sells, revenue splits automatically between creator, curator, and a 10% buyback pool that strengthens the ecosystem.
