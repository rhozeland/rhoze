Rhozeland is for creators of all levels — musicians, videographers, photographers, designers, engineers, influencers, and anyone building a creative brand. Whether you're starting out or scaling up, we have the tools and community for you.

Membership includes access to our studio spaces, creative services, workshops, networking events, and the Rhozeland Creator OS platform for booking studios, hiring talent, and managing projects.

Credits are the currency within Rhozeland's ecosystem. They can be earned through work, purchased, or received through $RHOZE token holdings. Credits unlock services, studio time, and talent hiring on the platform.

$RHOZE is Rhozeland's official token built on Solana. It powers every booking, hire, and collaboration on the platform. Revenue from clothing drops, the marketplace, and services flows back through buybacks, burns, grants, and causes — creating a deflationary flywheel that benefits the entire community.
