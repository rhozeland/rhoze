The Rhozeland homepage uses a two-pillar architecture (chosen 2026-06-22 over media-first and toggle alternatives). Below the hero, `src/components/rhoze/BrandPillars.tsx` renders two equal-weight cards:

- Pillar 01 · Rhozeland Media — artists, podcast (Angry Mortgage), vlogs/series, livestreams, brand partners, services. Solid white-on-dark "Enter media hub" CTA.
- Pillar 02 · Rhozeland Tech — Rhoze App (Beta badge), $RHOZE tokenomics, Community Leaderboard (Live badge), Web3/wallet. Outline "Access platform" CTA.

Keep this section compact, editorial, near-black on near-white. Do NOT introduce a Media|Tech nav toggle and do NOT move tokenomics off the homepage — both were considered and rejected in favor of the pillar layout.
