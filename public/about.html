Who we are

We fuel creators, grow their brands, and turn ideas into impact.

2016

The origin story

Rhozeland was born from a group of friends with a shared passion for creative arts and culture, starting as a small community built on collaboration and self-expression.

2021

Building the creative hub

We grew into a digital platform offering resources, connections, and services that helped creators bring their ideas to life, building a loyal network through our first Rhozeland hub.

2023

Studio launch

We opened our first physical studio space — a creative hub for recording, production, and collaboration under one roof.
